Small cents


Small cents were first minted in 1856 and were first put into curculation a year later in 1857. They are still minted to this day. There has been 6 designs that lasted more then a year. They are the following



Flying Eagle cent

The flying eagle cent was minted from 1856 to 1858. But they were only put into cerculation in 1857. On the obverse it had a eagle that was flying and on the reverse a reef with the words one cent

Indian Head cent

The Indian Head cent was minted from 1859 to 1909. It has a native American on the obverse and a reef with the words one cent on the reverse
